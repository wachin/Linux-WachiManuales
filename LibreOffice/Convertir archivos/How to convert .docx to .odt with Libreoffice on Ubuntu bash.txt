
How to convert .docx to .odt with Libreoffice on Ubuntu bash - Stack Overflow
https://stackoverflow.com/questions/21845789/how-to-convert-docx-to-odt-with-libreoffice-on-ubuntu-bash
Then you can use this command directly from command line

.docx to .odt
libreoffice --headless --convert-to odt *.docx

.doc to .odt
libreoffice --headless --convert-to odt *.doc

.doc to .txt
libreoffice --headless --convert-to txt *.doc

.odp to .txt
libreoffice --headless --convert-to txt *.odp
